Mathematical Background
=======================

We study the 1D infinite potential well.

The analytical solution is:

.. math::

    \psi_n(x) = \sqrt{\frac{2}{L}} \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right)

where:

- \( n \) is the quantum number
- \( L \) is the length of the well

The wave function describes the probability distribution of a particle
in a confined quantum system.

The infinite potential well is a fundamental model where analytical
solutions are known, making it ideal for benchmarking numerical methods.
